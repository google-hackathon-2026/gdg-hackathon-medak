## 7. Key Tips

1. **Email subject line matters** — Keep it short, mention "anketa" and "112"
2. **Include the form link PROMINENTLY** — top of email, not buried
3. **Mention the hackathon context** — this isn't a corporate product, it's a community project
4. **Respect that many deaf people communicate visually** — keep text simple, add emoji as visual markers
5. **Don't spam** — one email + one follow-up max per organization
6. **Weekend timing note:** Deaf clubs often have social events on weekends — posting Saturday evening may catch post-event scrolling
7. **GOGB interpreting service is 24/7** — they already handle emergency relay, so they deeply understand the problem. They're your #1 ally.
