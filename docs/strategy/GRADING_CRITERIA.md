# Grading Criteria — Build with AI 2026

## Problem
- Is the problem clearly defined?
- Is the defined problem relevant to the competition?
- **User feedback:**
  - Did the team conduct research involving real users?
  - Did the team take the research findings into account and adapt the application accordingly?
  - Did the team adequately address the identified problems?
- What is the potential for expansion beyond the local market?

## Technology
- The architecture and high-level components need to be defined. What Google technologies are used?
- Have all components been implemented?
- Has at least one problem encountered during implementation been highlighted, along with how it was resolved?
- Did the team present the actual use of the application (not a mockup)?
- How scalable is the solution in its current state?

## Business
- Are target personae and market need clearly defined?
- Did the team research the market and competition?
- Are there a sustainable monetization model and realistic revenue?
- What are the cost and resources needed for development?
- Who are the users? Who are the buyers?

## Delivery
- Is the presentation visually consistent?
- Does the presentation contain all of the necessary information? Is the presentation overcrowded?
- Does the pitch contain all of the necessary information?
- How engaging was the pitch?
- Was the complete pitch delivered in time?
